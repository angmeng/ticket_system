Pulau Payar Marine Park Day Trip

Super Fast Ferry Ventures

SFF offers you a fascinating one-day tour of Pulau Payar Marine Park. Nestled amongst a cluster of tropical islands that lie at the northern end of the Malacca Straits, off the Kedah Coast, Pulau Payar is haven of pristine and natural beauty. Gazetted as a marine park since 1985, this tranquil islan is reputed to have the largest number of coral species in the country. Not surprisingly, it is one of the favourite havens for snorkelling and scuba diving in Malaysia.

A high speed, fully air-conditioned ferry will take you there in 1 hr 30 mins.
